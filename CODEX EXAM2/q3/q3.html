Your Name is a 2016 Japanese animated film about two high school students who mysteriously start swapping bodies:



Mitsuha is a high school girl from a small town in the Hida region of Japan who dreams of leaving for Tokyo. Taki is a high school boy from Tokyo who works part-time in an Italian restaurant and wants to be an artist or architect. One day, they wake up to find themselves in each other's bodies, and they start to leave messages for each other on their phones. They eventually develop feelings for each other, but things get complicated when they try to meet in person.
